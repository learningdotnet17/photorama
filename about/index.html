Hello! My name is Brijesh Chawla and I made this website. I like to play with my camera and lenses and pretend I am a photographer.

A lot of people ask me if I have a YouTube channel (no, I do not) or an Instagram account (yes, I do! @brijeshchawla17). I find it easier to give them my website instead.

I like to shoot landscapes, cityscapes, nightscapes, long exposures, birds, and some macro (mostly for flowers). Waterfalls, people, not so much.

Currently (late 2020) I use a Sony A7rIII body with an assortment of lenses. I was in love with the Canon 16-35 f/4 lens for a while. These days I am loving the 35-70 focal length quite a bit.

I love shooting lighthouses, and sunsets, and lighthouses at sunsets. Love water bodies of any kind - lakes, rivers, ponds, oceans.

Hope you like these pictures. Drop me a line to say hi. Or follow me on Instagram.
